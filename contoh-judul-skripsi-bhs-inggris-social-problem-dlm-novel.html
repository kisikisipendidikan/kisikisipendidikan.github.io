Biographical text is a text explaining the life of famous people. It usually contains the timeline of his/her life and various events happened to him which are divided in chapters. Here's the sample of it. 

When people talk about modern beautiful football, Guardiola's name is surely inevitable. The current manager of Manchester City is closely related to the creation of tiki-taka and the great success in his former club, Barcelona, both as a player and manager. 
Born in Santpedor, Spain, on 18th January 1971, Pep Guardiola started his career as a footballer by joining the Barcelona football academy in 1984. His magnificient and incomparable skill as a midfielder led him to have an early debut in the senior team in 1990 and was apart of golden years of Azulgrana (Barcelona's nickname) in early 90s, including winning their first Champions League in 1992 under the genius Johan Cruyff, who was Pep mentor as well in developing attractive game and tactics. He stayed in Barcelona till 2001 before moving to AS Roma and retired as a player in 2006. 
His contribution for Spain national team was memorable too as he was the captain of the U-23 team when they won gold medal in Olympic Games 1992. He was also apart of the world cup squad in 1994 and 1998.  
Meanwhile, his managerial career began in 2008 when he was appointed by the club for leading junior team. Suddenly, he reached his stardom a year later after winning six titles in one season for the senior team and introduced the inspiring tiki-taka style with 4-6-0 formation. He stayed in Catalunya till 2004 and took a sabbatical year. Pep finally returned in 2013 once he was signed by Bayern Munich. Unfortunately, his success in Germany was only considered moderate. No wonder that he eventually decided to move to Manchester City in 2017 after finishing his work for Bayern for 3 years. He is now leading The Citizens to win their Premier League title this season.

39. berikan masing masing 2 contoh dari manifest social problem dan latent social problem​


Masalah sosial secara umum dibagi menjadi dua yaitu manfiest social problem dan latent social problem. Manifest social problem adalah suatu masalah yang terjadi di masyarakat dikarenakan ketimpangan antara nilai sosial dengan norma sosial, namun masyarakat masih bisa mengatasi permasalahan tersebut.  

Latent social problem adalah masalah sosial yang terjadi akibat ketimpangan nilai dan norma sosial, namun masyarakat sudah tidak mampu mengatasi permasalahan tersebut. Dampak dari akibat masalah sosial adalah konflik sosial, meningkatnya angka kriminalitas, kemiskinan.

Pembahasan

Contoh-contoh dari manifest social problem dan latent social problem.

Manifest social problem :

Konflik sosial disuatu daerah

Konflik sosial yang pernah terjadi diprovinsi lampung, namun sudah diselesaikan permasalahan tersebut

Konflik, bentrok daerah satu dengan lainya.

Tawuran yang sering terjadi dijakarta, bisa diselesaikan melerai pihak-pihak yang berkonflik dan tawuran selesai.

Latent social problem :

Kesenjangan pembangunan suatu daerah

Pembangunan kesenjangan disuatu daerah oleh pemerintah terhadap daerah tertentu, sehingga memunculkan kecemburuan sosial bagi daerah lainya. Karena pembangunan kesenjangan daerah tidak bisa dilakukan kepada  semua daerah, namun bertahap dan satu persatu

Korupsi, kejahatan, kemiskinan

Kejahatan manusia ini tidak bisa dibersihkan menyeluruh di dunia, karena manusia memiliki sifat baik dan buruk. Dampaknya tidak langsung dirasakan langsung oleh masyarakat, bertahap.
