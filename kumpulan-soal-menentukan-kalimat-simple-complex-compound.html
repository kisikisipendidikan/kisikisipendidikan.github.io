2. Minta bantuannya tugas soal Bahasa inggris : Simple Sentence, Compound Sentence, Complex Sentence, Compound-Complex Sentence. Kalimatnya harus sama


Jawaban:

Simple sentence: I sleep.

Compound sentence: I am sleepy, so I sleep.

Complex sentence: Because I am sleepy, I sleep.

Compund-complex: Because I am sleepy, I sleep, and it's 11 PM.

Penjelasan:

Independent clause(Main clause): Kalimat yang bisa berdiri sendiri. Contoh: She buys a car. [S + V1 + O]

Dependent clause: Kalimat yang tidak bisa berdiri sendiri. Biasanya diikuti oleh conjunction. Contoh: Because he was hungry. [Because » conjunction, he was hungry (S + to be + C) ].

Simple sentences: Terdiri dari 1 independent clause. Contoh: I play the ball. [S + V1 + O]Compound sentences: Terdiri dari 2 independent clause, dan coordinating conjunction (for, but, and, so, yet). Contoh: I am hungry, but I don't have time to eat. [I am hungry » independent clause (S + to be + C), but » coordinating conjunction, I don't have time to eat » independent clause (S + V) Complex sentences: Terdiri dari 1 independent clause dan minimum 1 dependent clause. Contoh: While I was walking, I saw a great view. [While I was walking » dependent clause, I saw a great view » independent clause]Compound-complex sentences: Terdiri dari compound sentence dan complex sentence. Contoh: I love eating, but when I am full, I don't want to eat. [I love eating » independent clause 1, but » coordinating conjunction, when I am full » dependent clause, I don't want to eat » independent clause.] Sehingga: [I love eating, but » compound sentence, when I am full, I don't want to eat » complex sentence]

Macam-macam kalimat di bahasa Inggris:
1. Simple sentence ( 1 subject + 1 verb ) Objects or adverbs are optional
    I run a printing business. (S + V + O)
2. Compound sentence (compound subjects or compound verbs)
    I'm answering phone and typing emails at the same time (double verbs)
3. Complex sentence (1 dependent clause + 1 independent clause)
    I left my laptop at the office because my boss wanted to check my
    work.         
    (conjunction = because)
4. Compound Complex sentence (complex sentence with compound verbs or nouns)
    I left my laptop and folders because my boss and his assistant wanted to check my work.

13. penjelasan lengkap simple, compound dan complex sentence


Pengertian dan Contoh Kalimat Simple Sentence dan Arti
Namanya juga sederhana (simple), jenis kalimat ini hanya terdiri dari 1 kalusa yang terdiri dari Subject dan Predicate. Meski hanya 1 klausa, kalimat ini sudah ememiliki arti yang utuh. Makanya 1 klause itu disebut Independet (berdiri sendiri), alias tidak butuh kalusa yang lain agar maksud dan artinya bias dipahami.
Example :
1. Some people like to go to market.

Pengertian dan Contoh Kalimat Compound Sentence dan Arti
Seperti dari namanya, compound artinya gabungan. Ini berarti kalimat compound adalah kalimat panjang yang terdiri dari gabungan 2 atau lebih klausa. Yang perlu dicatat disini adalah setatus kedua klausanya memiliki kemandirian sendiri sendiri. Artinya seumpama 2 kalusa itu dipisah, masing masih bias dipahami arti dan maksudnya. Ke 2 klausanya disebut sebagai Independent Clause.
Example :
1. I tried to study Math, and my friends tried to study English.

Pengertian dan Contoh Kalimat Complex Sentence dan Arti
Compex artinya ruwet alias tidak sederhana. Tentunya kalimat jenis ini terdiri dari beberapa klausa mesk begitu proses mixin klausa tersebut tidak sama seperti pada kalimat Compound.
Kalimat complex terdiri dari 2 atau lebih klausa yang mana salah satunya menjadi induk dan lainnya menjadi sub. Hal ini berarti, sub clause tidak akan memiliki arti yang sempurna senadainya dipisah dari induk (main clause).
Example :
1. The students are doing the homework because they will submit it tomorrow.

17. pengertian simple ,compound dan complex sentences dan contoh kalimat nya


1.
Simple sentences are sentences with only one clause and has the most basic structures of a sentence. (kalimat yg hanya memiliki satu klausa dengan struktur kalimat paling dasar)

contoh : Jessica cooked dinner early for her husband.

2.
compound sentences are sentences that are made up of two independent clauses that are connected with coordinating conjunctions. (kalimat majemuk setara)

contoh : Jessica cooked dinner early for her husband, but he came home late.

3.
complex sentences are sentences that are made up of one independent clause and one dependent clause that are connected with subordinating conjunctions. (kalimat majemuk bertingkat)

contoh : Jessica cooked dinner early for her husband, although he came home late.

24. The differences between simple, compound, complex, and compound-complex sentences in English (support you idea using your own sentences)? ​


The differences between simple, compound, complex, and compound-complex sentences in English are based on the number and types of clauses they have. A clause is a group of words that has a subject and a verb. Here are some examples of each sentence type:

A simple sentence has only one independent clause, which can stand alone as a complete thought. For example:

She smiled. (subject + verb)

He likes pizza. (subject + verb + object)

A compound sentence has two or more independent clauses, which are joined by a comma and a coordinating conjunction (such as and, but, or, so, etc.). For example:

She smiled, and he laughed. (two independent clauses joined by a comma and and)

He likes pizza, but she prefers pasta. (two independent clauses joined by a comma and but)

A complex sentence has one independent clause and one or more dependent clauses, which cannot stand alone and depend on the independent clause for meaning. Dependent clauses are introduced by subordinating conjunctions (such as after, because, who, where, etc.). For example:

She smiled when she saw him. (one independent clause + one dependent clause introduced by when)

He likes pizza because it is cheesy. (one independent clause + one dependent clause introduced by because)

A compound-complex sentence combines a compound sentence and a complex sentence. It has two or more independent clauses and at least one dependent clause. For example:

She smiled, and he laughed when they heard the joke. (two independent clauses joined by a comma and and + one dependent clause introduced by when)

He likes pizza because it is cheesy, but she prefers pasta since it is healthier. (two independent clauses joined by a comma and but + two dependent clauses introduced by because and since)

29. apa perbedaan simple sentence, compound sentence, complex sentence, compound complex sentence?


Punctuation (Tanda Baca) pada Compound-Complex SentencePada dasarnya, compound-complex sentence mengikuti aturan penggunaan tanda baca yang berlaku terhadap compound dan complex sentence. Secara ringkas aturannya adalah sebagai berikut.Compound SentenceComplex Sentence
compound
Jika menggunakan coordinate conjunction (for, and, nor, but, or, yet, so), tanda baca koma digunakan tepat sebelum kata hubung tersebut.Jika menggunakan conjunctive adverb (furthermore, however, otherwise, when, etc), tanda baca semicolon (titik koma) digunakan tepat sebelum kata hubung dan koma tepat setelah kata hubung.Dapat menggunakan semicolon untuk menghubungkan dua independent clause yang sangat berhubungan.

34. English text tentang informatika atau komputer yg mengandung ketiga jenis kalimat: simple sentence, compound sentence, dan complex sentenceEnglish text tentang informatika atau komputer yg mengandung ketiga jenis kalimat: simple sentence, compound sentence, dan complex sentence


Jawaban:

Computer is one of the vital tools for humans' daily work. Back in the day, a computer was a gigantic pile of machinery and they cannot be moved to another location. Today, computer is more compact and efficient which we can use it to work anywhere and anytime.

Penjelasan:

Simple Sentence merupakan kalimat yang memiliki satu Independent Clause dimana klausa tersebut tidak memerlukan klausa lainnya untuk menjadi kalimat penuh. Contoh: Computer is a one of the vital tools for humans' daily work.

Compound Sentence merupakan kalimat yang memiliki dua atau lebih Independent Clauses. Biasanya untuk menghubungkan dua klause terdapat linking word atau Coordinating Conjunction yaitu for, and, nor, but, or, yet, dan so (FANBOYS). Contoh: [A computer was a gigantic pile of machinery] and [they cannot be moved to another location.]

Complex Sentence merupakan kalimat yang memiliki satu Independent Clause dan satu atau lebih Dependent Clause. Dependent Clause merupakan klausa yang memerlukan Independent Clause untuk menjadi kalimat penuh. Dependent Clause biasa diawali which, where, when, because, therefore, dan lain-lain. Contoh: [Computer is more compact and efficient] which (we can use it to work anywhere and anytime.)

[...] = Lambang klausa independen.

(...) = Lambang klausa dependen.

Garis bawah = Lambang konjungsi.

Note: Boleh ditambah lagi atau dikurangi teksnya.

Ex : i eat pizza
i = sbg subjek  ,  eat = sbg predikt  , pizza = sbg objek

Compound Sentence : sederhanany kalimat yg terdiri dr 2 bagian, dan dihubungkan dg kata penghubung
Ex : I love pizza BUT, i can't eat it too much
Bag 1 = i love pizza    ,    bag 2 = i can't eat it too much    ,    BUT = kata penghub
mcam2 kata penghub = but, or, because, and, dll

1. subjekny dan/atau objekny diperjelas adalah adjective clause
   ex : i eat pizza which i bought yesterday
