6. If a rectangle has a perimeter of 28 meters and a width of 5 meters, find the length of the rectangle.


The length of the rectangle is 9 meters.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Pembahasan

(Explanation)

To find the length of the rectangle, we first need to know the formula of the perimeter of a rectangle, which is:

p = l + w + l + w or  p = 2(l + w)

where:

l = length w = width

Since we know that the width of the rectangle is 5 meters and the formula of the perimeter of a rectangle is 2(l + w), we can find the length of the rectangle by using the formula as follows.

⇒   p = 2(l + w)

⇒   28 = 2(l + 5)

⇒   28 = 2(l) + 2(5)

⇒   28 = 2l + 10

⇒   -2l = -28 + 10

⇒   -2l = -18

⇒   l = -18/-2

⇒   l = 9

Hence the length of the rectangle is 9 meters.

P = 2(20m + 10m) = 60m

The path made of ceramics has a width of 1 meter, so the area of the path is:

A = 1m x 60m = 60m²

To calculate the total cost, we need to multiply the area of the path by the cost per square meter of ceramics:

32. A rectangle has a perimeter of 42 meters and a length of 13 meters. Find the width of the rectangle.


The width of the rectangle is 8 meters.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Pembahasan

(Explanation)

The formula of the perimeter of a rectangle is p = length + width + length + width. It can be simplified to p = 2(length + width) or p = 2(l + w). As we know the length of the rectangle and the formula of the perimeter of a rectangle, we can use the formula to find the width of the rectangle as follows.

⇒   p = 2(l + w)

⇒   42 = 2(13 + w)

⇒   42 = 2(13) + 2(w)

⇒   42 = 26 + 2w  

⇒   -2w = 26 - 42  

⇒   -2w = -16

⇒   w = -16/-2

⇒   w = 8

Therefore, the width of the rectangle is 8 meters.

34. The perimeter of a rectangle is 84 cm. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than 3 timesthe width. a. Find the mathematical model b. Determine length and width c. Determine the area of a rectanglePlease Ans​


Pembahasan

Persegi panjang adalah bangun datar yang memiliki 4 sisi (dua pasang sisi sama panjang), 4 titik sudut, 4 sudut siku-siku di setiap sudutnya.

Luas persegi panjang merupakan perkalian dari panjang dan lebar persegi panjang. Luas persegi panjang dinotasikan dengan :

[tex]\boxed{A_{\text{rectangle}} = p \times l} \\ \\ [/tex]

p adalah panjang sisi persegi panjang, sedangkan l adalah lebar persegi panjang.

Keliling persegi panjangnya adalah dua kali jumlah panjang dan lebar sisi persegi panjang.

[tex]\boxed{K = 2(p + l)} \\ \\ [/tex]

Formula for perimeter and area of a rectangle

[tex]\boxed{P_{\text{rectangle}} = 2(l + w)} \\ \\\boxed{A_{\text{rectangle}} = l \times w} \\ \\ P \: \: \text{is perimeter of a rectangle} \\ \\ A \: \: \text{is area of a rectangle} \\ \\ l \: \: \text{is length of a rectangle} \\ \\ w \: \: \text{is width of a rectangle} \\ \\ [/tex]

Diketahui :

The perimeter of a rectangle is 84 cm. The length of a rectangle is 5 cm more than 3 times

the width.

Ditanya :

a. The mathematical model

b. Length and width

c. The area of a rectangle

Jawaban :

a. The mathematical model

[tex]P = 84 \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ [/tex]

b. Length and width

[tex]P = 2(l + w) \\ \\ P = 2(3w + 5 + w) \\ \\ P = 2(4w + 5) \\ \\ P = 8w + 10 \\ \\ 84 = 8w + 10 \\ \\ 8w = 84 - 10 \\ \\ 8w = 74 \\ \\ w = \frac{74}{8} \\ \\ \boxed{w = 9.25 \: cm} \\ \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ l = 3(9.25) + 5 \\ \\ l = 27.75 + 5 \\ \\ \boxed{l = 32.75 \: cm} \\ \\ [/tex]

c. The area of a rectangle

[tex]A = l \times w \\ \\ A = 32.75 \times 9.25 \\ \\ \boxed{A = 302.9375 \: {cm}^{2}} \\ \\ [/tex]

Kesimpulan :

a. The mathematical model

[tex]P = 84 \\ \\ l = 3w + 5 \\ \\ [/tex]

b. Length and width

[tex] \boxed{w = 9.25 \: cm} \\ \\ \boxed{l = 32.75 \: cm} \\ \\ [/tex]

c. The area of a rectangle

[tex]\boxed{A = 302.9375 \: {cm}^{2}} \\ \\ [/tex]

36. The length of a rectangle exceeds its width by 2 cm. If each dimension were increased by 3 cm, the area would be increased by 51 cm2. The width of original rectangle is equal to


The original width of the rectangle is equal to 6 cm.

[tex]\\[/tex]

Pembahasan

(Explanation)

We know that from the problem,

the length (l) of the rectangle exceeds its width (w) by 2 cm, so the length of the rectangle is width + 2 or w + 2,each dimension of the rectangle were increased by 3 cm so that the area would be increased by 51 cm², andthe new area of the rectangle minus the original area of the rectangle would be 51 cm².

The area of a rectangle is equal to the length multiplied by the width. Since the length of the rectangle exceeds its width by 2 cm, we can express the original area of the rectangle as OA = w(w + 2) where OA is the original area of the rectangle.

The new area of the rectangle is equal to the original area of the rectangle plus 51 cm² when each dimension of the original area is increased by 3 cm; we can express this as NA = (w + 3)(w + 2 + 3) or NA = (w + 3)(w + 5) where NA is the new area of the rectangle.

By using the information we got before, we can find the original width of the rectangle as follows.

NA - OA =  51 cm²

(w + 3)(w + 5) - w(w + 2) =  51

w² + 5w + 3w + 15 - w² - 2w =  51

w² - w² + 5w + 3w - 2w + 15 =  51

6w + 15 =  51

6w =  51 - 15

6w =  36

w =  36/6

w =  6

Therefore, the original width of the rectangle is equal to 6 cm.

40. The Length of a rectangular is three time the width of it.The area of the garden is 48m square.find the dimension of the garden


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Misalkan Panjang = P dan Lebar = L

P = 3L

Area = 48 m²

Area = P×L = (3L)(L) = 3L²

⇒ 3L² = 48

⇒ L² = 16

⇒ L = 4 m (Tidak mungkin lebar negatif)

Diperoleh P = 3×4 = 12 m

Maka, dimensi kebun: Panjang = 12 m dan Lebar = 4 m
